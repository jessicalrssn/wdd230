Discover Beautiful Bear Lake!

Bear Lake Views

From the mountains to the bright blue water, Bear Lake is not short on beautiful views.

Raspberry Days

Bear lake is famous for raspberries. Indulge in a famous raspberry shake and visit Bear Lake during our annual Raspberry Days, typically held in early August.

Water Sports

Bear Lake is called the Carribean of the Rockies for a reason. Get out on the water and try some of the awesome watersports available.

ATV's

Get off the beach and rent an ATV to see some amazing views in the mountains of Bear Lake.

Restaurants

Bear Lake has a variety of fun and tasty restaurants to choose from. You can't go wrong choosing where to eat.

Mountain Biking

Get back into the mountains and discover some amazing bike trails along the way.

Family Fun

Discover fun for the whole family with activities including the new ropes course in the canyon and go cart racing in garden city.

Bear Lake Marina

The perfect place to launch your boat or just admire the sail boats in the harbor.

Shopping

You can visit many shops around the area and find some unique gifts for loved ones or yourself.
