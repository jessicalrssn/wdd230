Discover Bear Lake!

Bear Lake Views

from the mountains to the bright blue water, Bear Lake is not short on beautiful views.

Raspberry Days

Bear lake is famous for raspberries. Indulge in a famous raspberry shake and visit Bear Lake during their annual Raspberry Days, typically taking place in early August.

Water Sports

Bear Lake is called the carribean of the rockies for a reason. get out on the water and try some of the awesome watersports available.

ATV

Get off the beach and rent an ATV to see some amazing views in the mountains of Bear Lake

Enjoy a Restaurant

Bear Lake has many fun and tasy restaurant to choose from. you cant go wrong choosing what to eat for dinner.

Mountain Biking

Get back into the mountains and discover some amazing biking trails along the way.

Activities

there are many other activities to partake in including the new ropes course and go carts in garden city.

Marina

This is where all your boating needs are met. launch your boat or admire the awesome sail boats in the harbor.

Bears

Its not rare to see carved bears all over from inside stores to in the park. how many can you find?
