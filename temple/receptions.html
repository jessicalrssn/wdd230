Receptions!

Temple Inn & Suites is happy to offer wedding reception accommodations! We have receptions halls in each of our hotels that can be easily customized to your needs. We also offer a simple catering menu or access to the kitchen for food preperation and serving.

Reception Offerings

  • Reception hall: Our beautiful reception hall has an open floor plan and can be set up in a variety of layouts to fit your needs. capacity: 140-200 people depending on location.

  • Decor options: We have a variety of simple decor items in many color options to make your reception unique to you.

  • Set up and take down services: Our staff is available to set up and take down tables and decor.

  • Simple catering Menu: We offer a light refreshment menu to be catered by the hotel.

  • Access to kitchen for food prep and serving: We allow you tobring your own refreshments or outside catering and give access to the kitchen for those needs.

  • Technology: A sound system, microphones, and a projection screen is included in the reception hall.
