Long Stay Accommodations!

Temple Inn & Suites has lovely suites perfect for a long-term stay. Full time temple missionaries can enjoy a comfortable stay in our kitchenette suites for the duration of their service.

Long-term Stay Offerings

  • Our best rooms & suites for missionaries: We offer our finest rooms and suites at a discounted price for couples serving a mission

  • A kitchenette in every suite: Every suite comes equiped with a kitchenette to prepare meals.

  • Laundry services: All long-term stay guest have access to the laundry room. Staff can do takeout laundry service for a small fee.

  • 24 hour access to computers: Long-term guests have 24 hour access to computers in the business center and family search center to better help you serve.

  • Transportation services: We offer a free shuttle to and from the temple. We also offer transportation to anywhere else you might need for a small fee.
