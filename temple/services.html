Services We Offer!

Temple Inn & Suites is here to serve you! Along with regular hotel benefits, we offer a variety of unique services tailored to your needs as temple goers. We are happy to help our guests with all their special needs.

Free Amenities

  • Free breakfast
  • Exercise/workout room
  • Indoor pool
  • WiFi high-speed internet access and smart TVs
  • Free parking
  • Temple Shuttle
  • Family history consultation and family ordinance card service

Specialized Services

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities
  • Childrens playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services
