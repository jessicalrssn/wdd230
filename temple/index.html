Featured Temple

Rome Italy Temple

The Rome Italy temple was the thirteenth temple to be built in Europe and the first built in italy. It serves church members in Italy as well as Malta, Greece, Cyprus, Albania and parts of Romania. The Rome temple was dedicated in March of 2019 and marked the first time in history when the entire first presidency and quorum of the twelve gathered to one location outside the United States. This beautiful temple has been a long awaited blessing for the saints in Italy and the surrounding areas.
