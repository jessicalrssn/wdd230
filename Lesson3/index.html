Overview

Site Name:

Bear Lake Chamber of Commerce

Purpose:

The purpose of the Bear Lake Chamber of Commerce website is to bring together the business community of the Bear Lake, Utah area. The chamber is dedicated to providing its members resources, training, networking opportunities, and advocacy with the local government. The chamber also works with local, state, and federal partners to grow recreational activities in the area to increase tourism and business prosperity.
